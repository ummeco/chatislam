## 2. AI Disclaimer — Read Before Using

**IMPORTANT:** ChatIslam uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate responses. AI responses are not fatwas (Islamic legal rulings). They are not the opinion of any specific scholar or institution. AI responses may contain errors, omissions, or outdated information.

For any matter of religious significance — including questions about halal and haram, marriage, divorce, inheritance, worship obligations, or any personal or community religious decision — please consult a qualified Islamic scholar.

## 3. Theological Correction Flow

ChatIslam operates a user-flagged correction process:

1. Users may flag any AI response they believe is theologically inaccurate using the flag icon below each response.
2. Flagged responses are reviewed by our moderation team within 5 business days.
3. Where the moderation team determines the concern has merit, the response is referred to a qualified Islamic scholar for review.
4. Corrections or annotations are applied to the response if the scholar-review confirms inaccuracy.

Filing a flag does not guarantee removal or correction. Flags that do not meet the threshold for theological inaccuracy (e.g., user preference for a different madhab) may be declined.
